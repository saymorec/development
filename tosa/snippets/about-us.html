About us

Tafadzwa Old Students Association (TOSA), is a group of former students of Tafadzwa Primary School who desire to give back to the school that made us who we are. We are constantly searching for opportunities to make a difference in the lives of current and future students.

An area that has attracted our attention, is the plight of needy children. We have selected a handful of students who need urgent attention. To these we provide: school uniforms, textbooks, and school fees. Resources permitting, we are looking forward to helping more students.
