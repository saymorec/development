About F & H Glass and Aluminium (Pty) Ltd

F & H Glass and Aluminium (Pty) Ltd is a manufacturer of high performance quality aluminium windows and doors for residential and commercial projects. Over the years we have steadily developed a reputation for technical competence, high quality and excellent customer service. Backed by Wispeco Aluminium and Crealco, our main aim is to meet your design expectations and as such our aluminium windows and doors are manufactured to your specification, ensuring a perfect fit all the time.

F & H Glass and Aluminium (Pty) Ltd strives to deliver excellent levels of quality, service and responsiveness, price competitiveness and technical support.
